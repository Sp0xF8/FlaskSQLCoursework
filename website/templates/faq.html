Frequently asked Questions

Here are some of our most commonly asked questions!

What is Horizon Travels? Horizon Travels is a travel agency that specialises in providing travel services to customers. While we are looking to expand to wider ranges of transport, we currently only provide flights. We offder multiple destinations and a wide range of flight times to suit your needs, we only offder national flights; however, with our price points, we have been connecting amilies for generations.

Why choose Horizon Travels? While we are a small company, we are well established in the national flight market. We offer our customers a wide range of destinations and at a price that is affordable for all. We are also a family owned business, so we understand the importance of family and the need to stay connected.
Due to our small footprint, we can also offer a more personal service to our flights and providing much greater customer service. We are inviting you to join the VIP experiance, hope to see you with us for your next flight!

Does Horizon Travels offer refunds? While we do not offer refunds, we do provide travel insurance for all flights booked through us! This means that, while you can not refund your own trip, if your flight is cancelled due to weather or other circumstances: you will be covered by our insurance and will be able to rebook your flight at no extra cost. We also offer a 24/7 customer service line, so if you have any questions or concerns, we are always here to help!

How do I ammend my booking? If you need to ammend your booking, you can do so by contacting our customer service team. We are available 24/7 and will be able to help you with any ammendments you need to make. We are also able to help you with any questions you may have about your booking.
If this is not an emergency, you can contact us via the contact form , or by emailing us directly!
